東南西北


Dong Nan Xi Bei is a project that aims to indirectly share and spread values, philosophy, and history through a gastronomic and cultural experience.

It is a work that was born with the intention of transmitting a full and rich culture. In which the eyes of the West have been left remained with the typical stereotypes, red dragons, giant pandas, red paper lanterns, and bamboo.

Therefore, through graphic design, it is intended to show a design totally out of stereotypes. Make an abstraction of Chinese philosophy and apply it both digitally, on paper and in space.
