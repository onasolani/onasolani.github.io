Resume here!

  • 2015

    I started to study Digital Product Design & Graphic Design at Eina, (University of Arts and Design of Barcelona) where I was able to improve my creative skills 💭.

  • 2017 – 2018

    Through the third year of the University, I participated in an International Contest (in collaboration with the company Game Solution Asia), in the Entrepreneur of the Year Contest by EY. I won the contest ✌️ and had the opportunity to work for Michael Kors and Cartier as UX/UI & digital designer in Shanghai for three months 🤩. It was an exciting experience. I was alone in a different country with a language that I didn't talk 🤯. Nevertheless, I learned a lot of Chinese, and I could improve the English level.

  • 2019

    Finally, finished the Degree 🎓, due to the unfavorable employment situation (not everything is so easy 😭), I decided to start working as a freelance while I was actively seeking an opportunity job because I hate to do nothing 💪.

    With patience, in October of 2019, I started my adventure at Memondo Network 🙌 as a UX/UI and Web designer, where I had the opportunity to manage the digital department and the social network as Creative Content with AR technology through JavaScript concept 🤓.

  • 2020

    Due to the pandemic 😭, in May of 2020, my contract was closed 😢, but I didn't give up 💪. I started to work as a freelance again while seeking another job opportunity & improving my skills with online courses in Neoland, or Google.

    In June, I had the pleasure to work in collaboration with Piaotiandi and Everis developer's team on an international project. As a result of working near them, I became more interested in the development and the potential that it has 🤩. So I started the Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp at IronHack 🚀 to leverage my skills to learn something new that makes me a more powerful designer and developer.
