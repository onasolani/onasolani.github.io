My work focuses on designing digital products and systems for B2B and B2C companies in different methodologies. Design Thinking (Doble Diamond), Agile, Scrum, and Design Sprint.

UX and UI design, with focus on prototyping and usability, information architecture (IA), wireframes, heat maps, UI animations and visual design.

Research part. Usability Testing (A/B & betas test), Benchmark study, Journey mapping and identifying opportunities with POW HOW NOW WOW, PoV, MoSCoW, and HMW methods.

As a UX writer, I seek to improve the user experience and degree of interaction with the product. It must be a concentrated language, a lot of meaning in a few words and serve both the company and the user.
